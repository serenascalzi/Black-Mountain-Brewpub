"When I get a little money, I buy books; and if any is left I buy food and clothes." –Erasmus

The Beverly Rogers, Carol C. Harter Black Mountain Institute at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas brings writers, and the literary imagination, into the heart of public life. Black Mountain Brewpub is a place for these writers to mix and mingle.

BMI hosts emerging and eminent writers and partners with a diverse array of institutions, beginning with the faculty and students of the Creative Writing Program International at UNLV. Other partners include The Writer's Block, Downtown Project, and Plympton in Las Vegas, as well as Archipelago Books, the Artist Protection Fund, the John W. Kluge Center at the Library of Congress, City of Asylum Pittsburgh, McSweeney's, and the International City of Refuge Network. In 2017, BMI became the new publishing home of The Believer.
